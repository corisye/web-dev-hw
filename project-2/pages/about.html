About the Project!

Hey there! My name is Cori and I love baths, good smellys, and relaxing. I think this project is awesome and super fun. And it's pretty easy to do. So grab some epsom salt and citric acid and let's turn that shitty rental tub of yours into a dang spa!!

So you may be wondering, what's the wonderful, fizzy magic that makes bath bombs so fun. The part that makes them fizz is sodium bicarbonate (baking soda) and citric acid. These chemicals are inactive when they are dry powders, but in water they dissolve and the citric acid reacts with the sodium bicarbonate to form carbon dioxide. The carbon dioxide is what makes the bubbles as the bathbomb breaks up and dissolves. This lets the perfumes and oils that make up the rest of the bath bomb release into the bathwater nourishing your skin and smelling up the room with delicious essential oils.

There are endless ways to customize your bathbombs. You can make them into any shape of mold you can find. You can add scent, color, glitter, dried flowers, or little trinkets that float up in the water for the kids! Have some fun with it! We hope you enjoy this luxurious project!
